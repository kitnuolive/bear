OUR
STORY

Beawelry reinvents the traditional perception of jewelry; from precious and valuable material to object of emotional representation. We strive to provide the most warming and beautiful experience of giving, receiving, and wearing.

Beawelry combines the delicacy of jewelry with the warmth of our Beawelry Bear(hence our name). The Beawelry Bear is copyright registered worldwide and designed specifically to take part in the giving experience by being a lovable doll, a delicate piece of jewelry, and a ring holder.

Beawelry provides premium gifts that can be personalised to create a new giving experience. We wish to help you express your emotions to your loved ones for all your special occasions. We invite you to bare your heart.
